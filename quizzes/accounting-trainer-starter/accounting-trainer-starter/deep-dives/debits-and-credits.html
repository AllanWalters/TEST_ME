Debits and Credits Deep Dive

Debits and credits do not mean increase and decrease by themselves. They mean left and right in the accounting system, and whether they increase or decrease an account depends on the account type.

Debit increases

Assets and expenses usually increase with debits.

Credit increases

Liabilities, owner's equity, and revenue usually increase with credits.

Memory help

Think of the accounting equation first, then decide which side of the equation the account belongs to.
