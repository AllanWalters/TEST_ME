Accounting Study Guide 01

This guide covers the accounting equation, account types, and the basic idea of debits and credits.

Accounting equation

Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity. This is the foundation of double-entry accounting.

Normal balances

Assets and expenses normally increase with debits. Liabilities, equity, and revenue normally increase with credits.

Study tips

  • Memorize the accounting equation.
  • Practice classifying accounts.
  • Say out loud which side increases each account type.
